Dubai chocolate ice cream bars

Home

Image by Akis Petretzikis

Description

Whip up the ultimate Dubai chocolate ice cream bars; the summer version of the viral Dubai chocolate! Chilled bars with shredded phyllo, pistachio praline paste, and a rich chocolate ice cream filling!

The following recipe is directly copied from the well-known Greek chef Akis Petretzikis. Check out his personal website for more Greek dishes!

Ingredients (for 12 pieces)

  • 150 g shredded phyllo dough
  • 40 g butter, + extra for the loaf tin
  • 230 gpistachio spread
  • 350 g chocolate ice cream
  • 30 g chocolate couverture, melted
  • 20 g pistachios

Steps

  1. Finely chop the shredded phyllo with a knife, untangle it well with your hands to spread it out, and set it aside.
  2. Place a frying pan over medium heat, add the butter, and let it melt.
  3. Add the shredded phyllo and lower the heat.
  4. Cook the phyllo for 7-8 minutes, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until it is nicely golden.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and set the shredded phyllo aside to cool for 15 minutes.
  6. Add the pistachio praline paste, mix them with a spoon, and set aside.
  7. Remove the ice cream from the freezer and set it aside for 10-15 minutes to slightly soften.
  8. Butter a 10x30 cm loaf tin, and then line it with parchment paper to cover the bottom and the sides, leaving quite an overhang.
  9. Add half of the shredded phyllo mixture to the loaf tin and spread it with a spoon.
  10. Add the ice cream on top and spread it evenly with the spoon.
  11. Add the rest of the shredded phyllo, spread it evenly, and press it down with the help of the parchment to flatten the surface.
  12. Put the loaf tin in the freezer for at least 2 hours until the shredded phyllo is firm and set.
  13. Remove the loaf tin from the freezer and unmold the dessert by flipping it onto a cutting board.
  14. Cut the dessert into about 12 bars, drizzle them with the melted chocolate, sprinkle with pistachios, and serve.
